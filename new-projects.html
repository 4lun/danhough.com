Projects Work with me

Like many developers, I tend to have at least a couple of projects going at any one time. In October 2013, I left my full-time job at Huddle in order to pursue a more flexible lifestyle working on these projects and my various hobbies more seriously. Below is a list of various projects I've been involved in. Some of them are ongoing, others are completed or came to a natural end.

StreetScout

Web and iOS Routing Application

StreetScout is a routing application for web and iOS which allows the user to find and visualise places on their routes which match a given query, such as coffee shops, pubs or grocery shops. Using Google Maps and Google Places data.

See more on the StreetScout website.
